A safe place for your mental health

At Remedy, we believe that makeup is more than just a cosmetic routine – it's a form of self-care, therapy, and an expression of self-love. We understand the power of makeup in nurturing your mental well-being and embracing your unique beauty. That's why we've created a brand that goes beyond the surface, providing you with products that not only enhance your external beauty but also uplift your inner self.

Founded in 2022 by Lucie Chiron-Nowbahari, a passionate makeup enthusiast, Remedy was born out of a personal journey towards self-acceptance and mental wellness. Lucie experienced firsthand how the transformative nature of makeup can empower individuals and boost their confidence. It became her mission to share this empowering experience with others, inspiring them to embark on their own self-care journey through makeup.

At Remedy, we want to redefine the way you perceive makeup. It's not just about following trends or hiding imperfections – it's about embracing your unique features and using makeup as a tool for self-expression and self-care. Our thoughtfully curated range of products is designed to cater to your individual needs, offering a blend of high-quality formulas and empowering messages.
